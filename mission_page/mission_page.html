We are KokiETech

____________________________________________

Black software engineers only make 4.9% of the software engineering workforce and Hispanic or Latino engineers only make 6.9%. Black engineers also have the lowest average salary at $84,962 and Hispanic at $88,050. KokyETech helps to serve the message to other software engineers as well as tech companies that people pf color are capable, hopefully resulting in improvements towards the workforce and wage gap percentages issues.



Services we offer

We offer users the option to browse through other peoples work, which will serve the idea that people of color are able of creating amazing products as software engineers. We serve the community by helping to create a community.

Contact us

The CEO's of KokiETech constantly their KokiETech and are posting regularly. They are available through github links you can find in your own feeds page. Feel free to contact them on their githubs!



Our Misson

connect with others
